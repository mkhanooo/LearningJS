These are the following events in Javascript:

1.  Click  (onclick)
2.  double-click (ondblclick)
3.  Right-click (oncontextmenu)
4.  Mouse-hover (onmousenter)
5.  Mouse-out (onmouseout)
6.  Mouse-down (onmousedown)
7.  Mouse-up (onmouseup)
8.  Key-press (onkeypress)
10. Key-up (onkeyup)
11. Load (onload)
12. Unload (onunload)
13. Resize (onresize)
14. Scroll (onscroll)
